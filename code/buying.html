About buying


Buying Property has become very easy now!
You are just one step away from finding a home of your dreams. Just search the type of home you want alone with the number of beds and baths you wish for in your dream house. You also need to specify the address of your desired property.These are the steps for buying a property:


  • Login! Register yourself if you dont have an account.
  • First select the Type of house you wish for in the form on the right side.
  • Select the home which you feel to know more about
  • If you wish to purchase or to know further details Click on Contact button.
  • Click on Purchase button once you have decided.
  • Go Through the entire purchasing procedure.
  • Check for all the legal documents related to the Property.
  • Decide a meeting with the owner at any particular time.
  • Finally meet the owner and finalize your deal!
